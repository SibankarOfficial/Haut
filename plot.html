How does Plots make money?

Hosting an event is completely free on Plots. Plots passes a transaction fee (10-15% + $0.40) onto its attendees. We also offer certain customized marketing services for select hosts.

What type of events can be hosted on Plots?

We welcome applications from all types of live experiences! We often work with hospitality groups, festivals, fashion brands, college organizations and always welcome small get-togethers to be hosted as well.

How does organic discovery on Plots work?

Verified hosts event's are displayed on a public map for thousands of event-goers to discover

How do I receive money on Plots?

Plots processes payments with Stripe. Simply fill out a an onboarding form, select your local currency and connect your bank account. Recieve payouts automatically within 48 hours.
